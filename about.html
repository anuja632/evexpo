Frequently Asked Questions

Auto & EV Tech Expo 2026 is a global showcase of automobiles, electric vehicles, components, batteries, charging solutions, innovations, and future mobility technology under one roof.

Manufacturers, distributors, dealers, investors, startups, traders, students, fleet owners, and EV enthusiasts are welcome to join and explore business opportunities.

Visitors can explore EVs, cars, bikes, charging stations, batteries, solar mobility, components, accessories, garage tools, automation systems, and upcoming future-tech models.

Exhibitors can register online, select booth type, submit company details, and confirm participation through our official expo website or organizing team.
